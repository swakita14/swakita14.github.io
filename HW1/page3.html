Program Information


Different Categories:


This program will be divided into three categories, beginner experienced and elite. In order to determinte which subcategory you lie, the table below should clarify and categorize where you belong. Knowing the category you belong in prevents injuries and maximizes the gains that can be attained from the training program.

Beginner:

Program includes numerous accessory exercise to focus on mind-muscle connection. Major goal is to build a strong and stable foundation for future muscle development
Intermediate:

Implements less acessory than beginner program. Includes heavier and more repeptition of the main three lifts
Advanced:

Contains little to none accessory movements. Program is based upon 75% to 80% of max weight. Takes severe toll on body if not coditioned well.


Phases in Training:


By identifying the categories, the lifter is able to choose from the different training schemes to produce the maximum potential

This nine week basic program is scale to all different categories of lifters with minor difference in sets and repetition of exercises. The base format of the program is summarized int three phases

  1. Muscular Conditioning
  2. Hypertrophy
  3. Linear Max
